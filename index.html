About Me
Nungki Maulidiyana

Hi, I'm Nungki Maulidiyana, children's illustrator and currently 21 yearS old. Art has always been my passion since I was a child, and I've been fortunate enough to turn that passion into a career pursuit. As an illustrator, I find joy creating colorful illustrations that bring stories. I believe that every stroke of the brush or pen holds the power to evoke emotions and ignite imaginations. I am constantly exploring new techniques and styles to enhance my craft, while also delving into the academic aspects of art and design. I am excited about the journey ahead, where I hope to continue growing as both an artist and an individual. Feel free to contact me for commissions, collaborations, or just to say hello. I look forward to connecting with you and bringing your creative projects to fruition.
